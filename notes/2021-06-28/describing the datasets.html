Relevant distributions of data

Since we want to sample from the spectrograms in order to augment our dataset (similar to taking crops of images to train image classifiers), we need to make sure that the spectrograms are on average large enough to sample from. Histograms of spectrogram lengths are in this directory.

This shows the lengths of the spectrograms in train. This shows the lengths of the spectrograms in test. This shows the lengths of the spectrograms in validation.

Files hist_A.png, hist_B.png, and hist_X.png shows the dist. of lengths broken up by label.
